# Gin Fizz
> *"You only make this on Thanksgiving"*

**Prep Time:** 5 minutes  
**Total Time:** 5 minutes  
**Servings:** 2-3 cocktails

---

## Ingredients
- 5 oz dry gin
- 6 oz half and half
- 1/3 cup powdered sugar
- 1/2 cup liquid egg whites (pasteurized)
- 2 oz fresh lemon juice
- 1.5 cups ice cubes
- Club soda (chilled)
- Orange peel for garnish

---

## Instructions
1. **Blend:** Add gin, half and half, powdered sugar, egg whites, lemon juice, and ice to blender. Blend until smooth and frothy.

2. **Pour:** Divide between glasses.

3. **Top:** Add splash of chilled club soda to each glass.

4. **Garnish:** Express orange peel oils over drink and drop in.

5. **Serve:** Immediately, while still frothy.

---

## Notes
- If the first batch doesn't work out, just drink it all and try again. The booze will let you enter a flow state.
