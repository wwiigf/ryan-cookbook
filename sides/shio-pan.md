# Shio Pan
> *"塩パン"*

**Prep Time:** 30 minutes  
**Rise Time:** 2.5 hours  
**Bake Time:** 15-20 minutes  
**Total Time:** 3+ hours  
**Yield:** About 10 rolls

---

## Ingredients
- 100g bread flour
- 300g all-purpose flour
- 60g sugar
- 1 tsp salt
- 5g instant yeast
- 1 cup warm milk
- 1 egg
- 5g olive oil
- 40g butter (for dough)
- Extra cold butter for rolling (about 100g)
- Coarse salt for topping
- Melted butter for brushing

---

## Instructions
1. **Mix dough:** Combine all dough ingredients (flours through 40g butter) in stand mixer bowl.

2. **Knead:** Knead with dough hook until smooth and elastic, about 8-10 minutes.

3. **First rise:** Let rise 2 hours until doubled.

4. **Portion:** Punch down dough and divide into 80g portions. Shape into balls.

5. **Shape:** Roll each ball into long oval.

6. **Add butter:** Place a stick of cold butter at one end of oval. Roll up tightly.

7. **Second rise:** Place rolls in baking pan. Rise 30 minutes.

8. **Prep for baking:** Spray rolls with water, sprinkle with coarse salt.

9. **Bake:** Bake at 375°F until golden brown, about 15-20 minutes.

10. **Finish:** Brush hot rolls with melted butter and serve.

---

## Notes
- The cold butter creates layers like a croissant
- The water spray helps the salt stick and creates a crispy crust
- Can freeze after shaping, before second rise
