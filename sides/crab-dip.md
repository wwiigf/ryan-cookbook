# Crab Dip
> *"Be a disgusting person and eat the whole thing yourself"*

**Prep Time:** 15 minutes  
**Bake Time:** 20 minutes  
**Total Time:** 35 minutes  
**Servings:** 6-8 (or 1 if you're committed)

---

## Ingredients
- 8 oz Philadelphia cream cheese, softened
- 1/2 cup Best Foods mayo
- 1 Tbsp fresh squeezed lemon juice
- 1/4 tsp hot sauce (or more to taste)
- 3 green onions, diced
- 2 cups fresh crab meat (broken into pieces, keep chunks)
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp pepper
- 3 Tbsp fresh grated parmesan (the good stuff)
- Sourdough baguette for serving
  

---

## Instructions
1. **Mix base:** Beat softened cream cheese and mayo until fluffy.

2. **Add acid:** Mix in lemon juice and hot sauce.

3. **Fold in crab:** Gently fold in green onions and crab. Don't overmix - keep those chunks intact.

4. **Season:** Add salt and pepper.

5. **Prep for baking:** Spread into baking dish. Top with grated parmesan.

6. **Bake:** Cook at 400Â°F for 20 minutes until bubbly and golden.

7. **Serve:** With warm sliced sourdough baguette.

---

## Notes
- Fresh crab is non-negotiable - canned won't cut it
- The chunks are important - don't shred the crab to bits
- This recipe makes enough for a party but no judgment if you eat it solo
- Best Foods mayo specifically - it makes a difference
- Double the hot sauce if you want a kick
