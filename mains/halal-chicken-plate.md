# Halal Chicken Plate
> *"For white people to enjoy something with some spices on it"*

**Prep Time:** 30 minutes  
**Cook Time:** 45 minutes  
**Total Time:** 1.5 hours  
**Servings:** 4

---

## Ingredients

### Chicken
- 2 lbs chicken thighs (trimmed and scored)
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tsp dried oregano
- 1 tsp ground black pepper
- 2 tsp salt
- 2 tsp curry powder (Madras preferred)
- 2 scoops Greek yogurt
- 1/2 cup mayo
- Juice of 1/4 lemon

### Rice
- 1.5 cups basmati rice
- 2.25 cups chicken stock (1:1.5 ratio)
- 1/4 white onion, chopped
- 1 bay leaf
- 1 tsp curry powder
- 1 tsp turmeric
- 1/2 tsp paprika
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Olive oil for pot

### White Sauce
- 1 cup mayo
- 1/2 cup Greek yogurt
- Splash white vinegar
- Juice of 1/4 lemon
- 1 tsp pepper
- 1 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp paprika

### For Serving
- Pita bread
- Shredded lettuce
- Diced tomatoes

---

## Instructions

### Marinate Chicken
1. **Mix marinade:** Combine garlic, oregano, pepper, salt, curry powder, yogurt, mayo, and lemon juice.

2. **Prep chicken:** Score chicken thighs deeply. Coat evenly with marinade. The mayo creates a fatty sear, scoring develops crispy craggles.

3. **Marinate chicken:** Refrigerate the marinaded chicken for up to 4 hours (max of 8)

4. **Grill:** Cook over fire to internal temp of 158Â°F. Let rest, then slice.

### Make Rice
4. **Sweat onion:** Heat olive oil in pot. Cook chopped onion until translucent.

5. **Toast spices:** Add curry powder, turmeric, paprika. Mix to coat onion. Cook 30 seconds until fragrant.

6. **Pearl rice:** Add rice, stir to coat with spices. Toast 2-3 minutes.

7. **Add liquid:** Pour in chicken stock and add bay leaf. Bring to boil.

8. **Cook covered:** Once boiling, reduce to low. Cover with towel-wrapped lid. Cook 18 minutes.

9. **Steam:** Turn off heat. Let steam additional 18 minutes without lifting lid.

10. **Fluff:** Remove bay leaf. Gently fluff rice with fork.

### Make White Sauce
11. **Mix sauce:** Combine mayo, yogurt, vinegar, lemon juice, and seasonings. Whisk until smooth.

### Assemble Plate
12. **Plate:** Bed of rice, sliced chicken on top, shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes. Drizzle white sauce over everything. Serve with warm pita.

---

## Notes
- The mayo in the marinade is key - it adheres better than oil and creates incredible char
- Scoring the chicken is crucial for crispy bits and flavor penetration
- The towel under the lid absorbs steam for perfect rice texture
