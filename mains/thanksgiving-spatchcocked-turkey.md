# Thanksgiving Spatchcocked Turkey
> *"It's either this, or burning down your house and loved ones in a fiery ball of death by frying a turkey"*

**Prep Time:** 30 minutes  
**Brine/Rest Time:** 4-5 days  
**Cook Time:** 45-55 minutes  
**Total Time:** 5 days  
**Servings:** 8-10

---

## Ingredients
- 1 (10 lb) turkey
- 1 cup butter, room temperature
- 5 Tbsp salt (plus 1 Tbsp for butter)
- 5 Tbsp pepper
- 2 tsp cajun seasoning
- 1 tsp paprika
- 2 tsp onion powder
- 1 tsp dried oregano
- Fresh parsley (4 sprigs)
- Fresh mint (2 sprigs)

---

## Instructions

### 6 Days Before Thanksgiving
1. **Start defrost:** Put frozen turkey in fridge to defrost for 3 days.

### 3 Days Before
2. **Spatchcock:** Remove backbone with kitchen shears. Flip and press flat to break breastbone.

3. **Dry brine:** Mix equal parts salt and pepper (5 Tbsp each). Liberally coat outside. Get hands under skin and season between skin and meat. Refrigerate for 1 day.

### 2 Days Before
4. **Make compound butter:** Mix room temp butter with paprika, oregano, onion powder, cajun seasoning, chopped parsley, and chopped mint.

5. **Butter the bird:** Rub butter mixture everywhere - under skin, over skin, in all crevices. Return to fridge for up to 3 days.

### Thanksgiving Day
6. **Roast:** Place in 425°F oven for 45-55 minutes until breast reads 145-150°F.

7. **Rest and carve:** Let rest 15 minutes, then carve.
