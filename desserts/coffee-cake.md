# Coffee Cake
> *"Ooey gooey and good"*

**Prep Time:** 20 minutes  
**Bake Time:** 45-60 minutes  
**Total Time:** 1.5 hours  
**Servings:** 12

---

## Ingredients

### Cake
- 2 cups buttermilk
- 1 tsp baking powder (for buttermilk)
- 4 cups flour
- 2 cups sugar
- 2 tsp baking powder (for dry mix)
- 1 tsp salt
- 1/2 cup shortening

### Cinnamon Topping
- 1 cup butter, melted
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1 Tbsp cinnamon
- 3/4 cup reserved cake batter
- Chopped pecans (optional but NEEDED)

---

## Instructions

1. **Prep:** Preheat oven to 350Â°F. Butter a 9x13" steel pan.

2. **Activate buttermilk:** Pour buttermilk into bowl. Add 1 tsp baking powder, mix well. Set aside to bubble.

3. **Mix dry:** In separate bowl, combine flour, sugar, 2 tsp baking powder, and salt.

4. **Add shortening:** Add shortening to dry mix. Mix until crumbly.

5. **Combine:** Add buttermilk mixture to dry ingredients. Stir until just incorporated - don't overmix.

6. **Reserve batter:** Remove 3/4 cup of dough and set aside for topping.

7. **Press base:** Press remaining dough into baking pan using wet spoon.

### Make Topping

8. **Mix topping:** Whisk melted butter, brown sugar, and cinnamon together.

9. **Add reserved dough:** Mix in the 3/4 cup reserved batter until combined.

10. **Top cake:** Pour topping over dough in pan. Use metal spoon to spread, then press mixture into the dough.

11. **Bake:** Bake 45-60 minutes until top is golden brown.

---

## Notes
- The pecans aren't actually optional - they make this cake
- Pressing the topping into the dough creates pockets of cinnamon goo
- The reserved batter in the topping creates crispy bits
- Keeps for days but best warm
