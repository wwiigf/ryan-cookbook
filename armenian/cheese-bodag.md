# Cheese Bodag
> *"Don't forget to complain about the phyllo dough"*

**Prep Time:** 30 minutes  
**Bake Time:** 20-25 minutes  
**Total Time:** 1 hour  
**Yield:** About 12-15 pieces

---

## Ingredients
- 1 package phyllo dough (frozen section)
- 2 lb Monterey Jack cheese, grated
- Italian parsley, chopped
- 3 eggs
- Salt to taste
- 1/2 cup melted butter

---

## Instructions
1. **Prep phyllo:** Defrost frozen phyllo in fridge overnight. Keep covered with damp towel while working.

2. **Make filling:** Mix grated cheese, chopped parsley, eggs, and salt until fully combined.

3. **Prep sheet:** Remove one sheet of phyllo. Tri-fold it lengthwise, then brush with melted butter.

4. **Add filling:** Spoon cheese filling at the bottom of the folded strip.

5. **Fold:** Flag fold (triangle fold) from bottom to top, like folding a flag.

6. **Repeat:** Continue with remaining phyllo and filling.

7. **Bake:** Place on lined baking sheet. Bake at 375Â°F for 20-25 minutes until golden and crispy.

---

## Notes
- The phyllo WILL tear and annoy you - it's tradition to complain about it
- Defrost in fridge, never on counter - soggy phyllo is worse than dry
- Can be frozen before baking - great make-ahead appetizer
